Imagine yourself staring at a PowerPoint presentation with this color combo. Does it make you want to scream? Does it feel like your eyes are gonna bleed? Blue on top of red (and even green on top of red) has this amazing effect. It makes the text look like it’s vibrating. Our eyes want to make the blue recess and the red pop out. Then, they want to make the blue pop out and the red recess. The phenomenon is called chromostereopsis. And it is enough to drive a person insane. So don’t ever design with blue or green text on red. Ever.
